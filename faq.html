FAQ

Certainly! Here is a list of frequently asked questions (FAQs) for a hotel:

  1. How do I make a reservation?
    - Mail us on info@gangesgrand.in.
  2. What is the check-in/check-out time?
    - Check-in time 1PM, and check-out time is 11 AM. Early check-in or late check-out based on availability on chargeable basis.
  3. Is parking available?
    - Yes, we offer basement parking.
  4. Is there Wi-Fi available?
    - Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.
  5. Do you have a restaurant on-site?
    - Yes, we have Multicusine DAWAT restaurant at 1st floor.
  6. Is room service available?
    - Yes, Room Service available from 7 Am to 10:30 Pm.
  7. Are pets allowed?
    - Not Allowed.
  8. Do you have a fitness center?
    - Not Available.
  9. Are smoking rooms available?
    - Yes.
  10. Do you provide airport shuttle service?
    - Yes, we offer airport pick-up on chargeable basis.
  11. Is there a pool on-site?
    - No.
  12. What amenities are included in the room?
    - Check Room description.
  13. Can I request additional amenities for my room?
    - Yes, we offer a range of additional amenities upon request. Please contact our front desk for details.
  14. What is your cancellation policy?
    1-If the booking will cancel before 07 Days of check-in date, No cancellation charge, The advance amount will be adjusting with future booking.
    2-If the booking will cancel with-in 07 Days of check-in date, 50% of advance Amount charge as cancellation fees, Rest will be adjusting with future booking.
    3- In case of NO SHOW, entire amount will be charge as cancellation.
  15. Do you have meeting/event facilities?
    - Yes, we offer GODAVARI Banquet for Social Get Together , Conference Rooms. Please contact our events team on 8303521654.
  16. Is there a nearby attraction or point of interest?
    • Lord Kashi Vishwanath Temple
    • Kaal Bhairav Temple
    • Dashaswamedh Ghat
  17. How can I contact the front desk or concierge?
    - You can reach our front desk or concierge by dialing [7235809898 / 05422392731/32] or 9 from your room.
  18. Is there a minimum age requirement for check-in?
    - Guests must be at least [21] years old to check in.
